Why is There an SHC?

The story behind the SHC in NatoTatoSHC is simple. I had a GitHub account called NatoTato at home. At school, I couldn’t access it due to email verification, so I created a new account meant to be called NatoTatoSCH (for school), but I misspelled it—and it stuck. I had done so much on NatoTatoSHC that I deleted the original.
